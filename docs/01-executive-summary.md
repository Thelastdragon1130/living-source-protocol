# Executive Summary

Living Source Coin (LSC) is a governance-first digital asset designed around a foundational principle:

Authority originates from the source, governance aligns through a root reference layer, and execution is carried out by utilities.

LSC is not just a payment token. It is the coordination layer for a system that integrates identity, governance, record integrity, and execution across digital and institutional environments.

The Living Source Protocol establishes:

- A clear origin of authority (the initiating party)
- A governance layer that preserves alignment and integrity
- Execution systems (AI, institutions, blockchain) that function strictly as utilities

This structure eliminates authority inversion, strengthens record-based truth, and enables transparent, scalable governance.

LSC functions as:

- A governance asset
- A participation unit
- A coordination token for ecosystem activity
- A bridge between human intent and system execution

The goal of LSC is to build systems where:

- The source is not lost
- The record is not corrupted
- The utility does not become the authority
- Governance remains transparent and enforceable
